How do you approach a new project? Walk me through the process

Here is my process:

Tell Me More

  1. First round of stakeholder interviews to define the problem and scope
  2. Second round of stakeholder interviews to optimize user experience
    Outcomes:
    • Outline of system and UI flow multiple use cases
    • Outline of system architecture
  3. Select appropriate technologies, platforms, and libraries to use
  4. Develop working prototype
  5. Gather feedback on prototype
  6. Prioritize remaining tasks based on feedback
  7. Refine the prototype
  8. Repeat steps 5 through 7 until the prototype is acceptable
  9. Deploy application to production
